Chunky Monkey Milkshake

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp. Nutella
  • 1 tbsp. Peanut Butter

Instructions

  1. Break or chop the frozen banana into small chunks
  2. Add the banana chunks and the milk into a blender.
  3. Add the Nutella and peanut butter. For best results, use a spoon that had been rinsed in hot water. Blend until smooth and adjust Nutella & peanut butter to taste.
